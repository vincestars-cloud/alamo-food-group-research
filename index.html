Our Story

It Started in the Kitchen.
It Started Out of Necessity.

In 2021, during the pandemic, our founder saw firsthand how hard it was for restaurants to get the products they needed — on time and at a fair price.

So he started Alamo Food Services as a mission to help fellow restauranteros survive and thrive. What began as supply for our own restaurants quickly grew as other owners asked for the same help.

Today, we serve restaurants, food trucks, schools, and businesses across the Southeast with quality products at the best prices.
