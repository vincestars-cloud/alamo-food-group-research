OUR STORY

It Started in the Kitchen. It Started Out of Necessity.

In 2021, during the pandemic, our founder saw firsthand how hard it was for restaurants to get the products they needed — on time and at a fair price.

So he started Alamo Food Services not as a side business, but as a mission to help fellow restauranteros survive and thrive. What started as a way to keep our own restaurants running quickly grew as other restaurant owners asked for the same help.

Today, we proudly serve restaurants, food trucks, schools, and businesses across the Southeast with quality products at the best possible prices.
